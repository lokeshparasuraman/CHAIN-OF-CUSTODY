Blockchain Verification

Verify the integrity of digital evidence using our blockchain-based chain of custody system. The blockchain ensures that evidence cannot be tampered with and provides a transparent audit trail.

About Blockchain Verification

This system uses a blockchain to create an immutable record of all evidence handling. Each block contains cryptographic hashes that link to previous blocks, ensuring the integrity of the entire chain. Any attempt to modify evidence would break the chain and be immediately detectable.
